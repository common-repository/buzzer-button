Become a Buzzer Button in different Sizes and Styles.

## Description
This plugin enables you to display the [Buzzer.Best](https://buzzer.best/) button on your site. The plugin can easy implement with a shortcode. [Shortcodes & Demo](https://klaetke.com/wordpress-buzzer-button)


### Usage 
Integrate the Shortcode [buzzer_button] ([All Shortcodes](https://klaetke.com/wordpress-buzzer-button#Shortcodes)) in WordPress and you get the Buzzer.Best in Spezial Mini Embed Version with Sound and Touch Effects and in different Colors. Easy to integrate. Use the Buzzer as Special CTA-Button or for Gamification in your Blog.
